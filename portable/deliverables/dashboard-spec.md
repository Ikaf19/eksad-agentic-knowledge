# Dashboard Specification

## Purpose

Contract for a dashboard/report build request, owned by Data Analyst and handed off to BI/FE/engineering as needed.

## Required sections

1. Audience and decisions supported
2. KPI catalog
3. Filters and dimensions
4. Time grain and refresh expectation
5. Data source mapping
6. Visualization intent
7. Access/sensitivity notes
8. Empty/error/no-data behavior
9. Acceptance criteria

## Readiness checklist

- [ ] KPI formulas are unambiguous.
- [ ] Dimensions and filters are named in business language.
- [ ] Data source ownership is known or flagged.
- [ ] Access/sensitivity constraints are explicit.
