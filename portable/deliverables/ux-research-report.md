# UX Research / Usability Report

## Purpose

Structured UX evidence and recommendation report produced by UI/UX Designer.

## Required sections

1. Research objective
2. Target users / roles
3. Source evidence or session notes
4. Task flow observed or reviewed
5. Findings by severity
6. Accessibility and usability risks
7. Design recommendations
8. Open questions and handoff owners

## Readiness checklist

- [ ] Findings are tied to user/task evidence.
- [ ] Recommendations are separated from confirmed requirements.
- [ ] Accessibility concerns are documented.
- [ ] BA/SA/FE/QA handoffs are explicit.
