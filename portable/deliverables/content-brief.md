# Content Brief

## Purpose

Source-grounded brief for content creation, review, and approval.

## Required sections

1. Audience
2. Objective
3. Channel/format
4. Tone and language
5. Approved source materials
6. Core message
7. Required inclusions
8. Explicit exclusions
9. Approval owner

## Readiness checklist

- [ ] Source materials are listed.
- [ ] Unsupported claims are excluded or flagged.
- [ ] Approval owner is named.
- [ ] Sensitive/unreleased content constraints are explicit.
