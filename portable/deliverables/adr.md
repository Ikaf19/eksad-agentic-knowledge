# ADR Contract

## Purpose

Define required sections, ownership, evidence, and quality gates for `adr` deliverables.

## Owner

See `portable/deliverables/deliverable-matrix.md`.

## Required properties

- Clear scope and assumptions.
- Traceability to upstream requirements or decisions.
- Explicit risks/open questions.
- Evidence links where applicable.
- Handoff notes for downstream roles.

## Validation

Use role-specific EKSAD standards and templates from `EKSAD/gpt/`.
