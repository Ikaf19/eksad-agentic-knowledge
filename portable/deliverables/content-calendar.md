# Content Calendar

## Purpose

Planning artifact for sequenced content production and approval.

## Required sections

1. Campaign/theme
2. Audience segment
3. Content item list
4. Channel
5. Planned date
6. Source/owner
7. Review/approval status
8. Publication gate

## Readiness checklist

- [ ] Each item has source and approval owner.
- [ ] Publication gate is explicit.
- [ ] No external publication is assumed by the draft.
